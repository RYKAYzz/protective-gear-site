About ARK Hygiene Solutions Kenya

Your premier partner in healthcare safety and hygiene solutions across Kenya and East Africa. We provide comprehensive protective equipment, medical supplies, and sterilization solutions to healthcare facilities, ensuring the highest international standards of safety, cleanliness, and infection control compliance.

Our Story

ARK Hygiene Solutions Kenya was established in 2018 with a clear mission: to provide healthcare facilities across Kenya and East Africa with reliable, high-quality personal protective equipment, medical supplies, and sterilization solutions. We understand the critical importance of safety in healthcare environments and are committed to supporting medical professionals with the tools they need to protect themselves, their patients, and maintain the highest standards of infection control.

Since our founding, we have built strong partnerships with over 500 healthcare facilities, including major hospitals, clinics, medical centers, and research institutions throughout Kenya. Our comprehensive product range includes certified PPE, medical waste disposal equipment, cleaning supplies, sterilization solutions, and specialized medical equipment - everything needed to maintain the highest international standards of hygiene and safety in healthcare environments.

We are proud to be ISO 13485 certified and maintain strict quality control standards. Our team of certified technicians and healthcare professionals ensures that every product meets or exceeds international safety standards and regulatory requirements.

Our Mission

To provide healthcare facilities across East Africa with reliable, high-quality protective equipment and hygiene solutions that ensure the safety of medical professionals and patients while maintaining the highest international standards of cleanliness, infection control, and regulatory compliance.
